Know Your Customer

Who's Buying

Homeowners who want a clean, showroom-look garage. Often triggered by seeing a neighbor's floor or a YouTube video. Emotional purchase — sell the transformation.

Budget Range

Standard 2-car (400–500 sq ft): $2K–$5K installed. 3-car: $3K–$7K. Most homeowners pick Tier 2 with flake. Upsell polyaspartic topcoat for the "wow" factor.

The Problems They Have

Home Depot epoxy failed. #1 objection. It peeled in 6 months. Water-based, 50% solids. Ours is 100% solids — totally different product.

Hot tire pickup. Car tires at 200°F peel cheap coatings. Homeowners don't know this until it happens.

Oil stains and dust. Bare concrete absorbs everything and creates dust constantly. Hard to sweep, impossible to clean.

Wasted space. An ugly garage is unused space. A coated garage becomes a gym, workshop, hangout, or showroom.
