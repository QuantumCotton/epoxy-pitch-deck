Selling Showroom & Retail Floors
Auto dealerships, boutiques, galleries, luxury retail, lobbies — first-impression floors
Know Your Customer

Who's Buying

Business owners, brand managers, interior designers, auto dealership GMs. They care about WOW factor, brand image, and customer experience. The floor IS the brand.

Budget Range

$6–$15/sq ft installed. Showrooms are 2,000–10,000 sq ft. Total: $12K–$100K+. These clients spend on image. Sell the metallic effects and designer finishes hard.

The Problems They Have

Boring concrete kills sales. Customers judge a business in 7 seconds. A grey concrete floor says "cheap" before anyone opens their mouth.

Yellowed floors look filthy. Big storefront windows = UV exposure. Standard epoxy yellows in months. The floor looks dirty even when clean.

High foot traffic wears fast. 500+ customers/day. Standard coatings dull and scratch within a year. Constant re-coating = lost revenue during closures.

Cookie-cutter look. Every competitor has the same VCT tile or polished concrete. A metallic epoxy floor is a statement piece they can't replicate.

How to Sell Each Tier
Tier 1 — Economy (Clean Base)
"Solid color, high-gloss finish that looks clean and professional. Great for stockrooms, service areas, or if you're getting the space ready to lease. Simple, effective, affordable."
Tier 2 — Professional (Designer Floor)
"This is our metallic epoxy — liquid metal effects that flow across the floor like molten silver. UV-protected so it won't yellow by your windows. Every floor is one-of-a-kind. Your customers will photograph it."
Tier 3 — Premium (Luxury Flagship) ★
"This is the auto-dealership, luxury-boutique, art-gallery floor. Ceramic-infused for diamond hardness. Polyaspartic topcoat gives the deepest, wettest mirror gloss in the industry. 15+ year beauty with zero maintenance."
