Know Your Customer

Who's Buying

Facility managers, property owners, retail chains, hospital administrators. They care about longevity, maintenance costs, and downtime.

Budget Range

$3–$15/sq ft installed. Most commercial clients budget $5–$8/sq ft. Upsell with lifespan math: "Tier B costs 30% more but lasts 3× longer."

The Problems They Have

VCT tiles crack, peel, and yellow. Constant waxing costs $0.15/sq ft every quarter.

Downtime kills revenue. Recoating takes 3–5 days. That's thousands in lost business.

Grout lines harbor bacteria. Healthcare and food service need seamless, sanitizable surfaces.

Cheap epoxy yellows near windows within months. Looks terrible in public-facing spaces.

How to Sell Each Tier
Tier 1 — Economy
"This is your workhorse. Standard epoxy, solid color, gets the job done for back-of-house areas — storage rooms, service corridors, anywhere aesthetics aren't priority."
Tier 2 — Professional
"This is our bread and butter. UV-stabilized so it won't yellow near windows, with a defoamer for a glass-smooth finish. We add ceramic microspheres for 40% more scratch resistance. Lasts 7–10 years in heavy traffic."
Tier 3 — Premium (Novolac)
"This is aerospace-grade chemistry. The Novolac resin resists chemicals, heat up to 350°F, and heavy impacts. Hospitals, malls, airports — this is the 15-year floor that pays for itself."
